HOW TO USE THE WEBSITE A GUIDE!
1. Browse Products
- Open the website home page
- Scroll through the product grid to see all available items
- Each product displays: image, name, price, and description

2. Filter by Category
- Click on category links in the navigation bar: **All**, **Electronics**, **Home**, or **Fashion**
- The product grid updates instantly to show only items in that category

3. Search for Products
- Type a product name, keyword, or category in the search box
- Results update in real time as you type
- Or click the **Search** button to apply your search

4. Add Items to Cart
- Click the "Add to cart" button on any product card
- A green confirmation message appears briefly
- The **Cart** counter in the top navigation updates with the new item count

5. View Your Cart
- Click the Cart (#) link in the top navigation
- See all items in your cart with quantity and price
- View the total amount due at the bottom

6. Remove Items
- On the cart page, click the red **"Remove"** button next to any item
- Item is deleted and cart total updates immediately

7. Checkout
- Click "Proceed to Checkout" button (only available when cart has items)
- Fill in: Full Name, Email, Shipping Address, Card Number
- Click "Place Order" to submit
- Success message confirms your order

8. Persistent Cart
- Your cart is saved automatically (in browser storage)
- Cart items remain even if you refresh or close the page
